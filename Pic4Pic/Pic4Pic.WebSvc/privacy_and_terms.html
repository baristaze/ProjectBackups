We might collect information about you from other users that you interact through our app; for example: feedback on you.

 

Other information we receive about you

We receive other types of information about you:

 

When you use our app, we receive your geo location information: latitude, longitude, current city, state, county, etc. This is mostly to suggest date candidates (other users) in a better way.

 

When you use our app, we receive your usage patterns and logs. For example, when you used our app, when and which buttons you tapped through our apps, which links you have clicked, which candidates you have visited, errors you your have received, crash logs, warning traces, etc. This helps us to improve user experience and optimize app features.

 

We also collect some basic information about your device. For example: Device make, model, version, operating system version, software development kit version, etc. Most of these are used to reproduce a problem when you faced in our test environment and statistical purposes.

 

We might collect more info in the future like your phone contacts, identity, etc. However, you will be asked for the permission either by our app or the by your phone’s operating system.

Your username, user id (a random text), short bio (including gender, profession, education level, etc.), detailed bio (authored by you), instant status, availability, your current city and state, your hometown city and state, pixelated versions of your photos, link to your profile are always publicly available. Deleting and deactivating your account

Although, delete and deactivate features haven't been released yet, we'd like to set our policy on them to let you know what to expect.

 

Deactivate

Deactivating your account puts your account on hold. Other users will no longer see your profile, but we do not delete any of your information. Deactivating an account is the same as you telling us not to delete any information because you might want to reactivate your account at some point in the future.

Your current friends/dates will still see you and your interaction with them.

 

When you delete your account, it is permanently deleted from our system. It typically takes about one month to delete an account, but some information may remain in backup copies and logs for up to 90 days. You should only delete your account if you are sure you never want to reactivate it.

 

Deletion

Certain information is needed to provide you with services, so we only delete this information after you delete your account. Some of the things you do via our app aren't stored in your account, like sending someone a message (where a previously interacted candidate may still have a message you sent, even after you delete your account). That information remains after you delete your account.
